Audio sermons a plenty...

February 20, 2020
I am quite the fan of listening to podcasts and whatnot as I drive around in my van, and I have recently been listening to various Dr. Martyn Lloyd Jones audio sermons. These are quite brilliant, and highly recommended. The MLJ Trust has made available over 1600 recorded sermons from the great man himself, all for free. There is also a mobile app available, which makes the collections easy to navigate, has a built in player, and has a significant introduction to each sermon.
