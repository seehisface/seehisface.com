The Chosen

Incredible

I have just finished watching the first series of The Chosen. Created by Dallas Jenkins, a Christian and a director, writer and film producer, the show is already The highest crowd-funded TV series or film of all time.

The following is from their site and pretty much sums up what it is about:

The Chosen is the first-ever-multi-season TV show about the life of Jesus. Created outside of the Hollywood system, The Chosen allows us to see Him through the eyes of those who knew him. No matter where you are at in your journey with Jesus Christ, this TV show is for you.

I cannot recommend this enough. All of the first season episodes are available from their YouTube Channel, just search for ‘Global Livestream’ on that page.

Once you have enjoyed this, I recommend helping to fund the next season and beyond. One way is to Pay it Forward.

I feel truly blessed to have experienced this show, and am very much looking forward to season 2. Thank you Dallas!
