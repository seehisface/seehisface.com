An Old William Lane Craig Debate

I like the beard!

Whilst browsing at some William Lane Craig videos yesterday, I came across this debate that I had not seen before.

It is from 1998 and he is debating with Dr. Keith Parsons, a Professor of Philosophy and a non-believer.

The debate was held at Prestonwood Baptist Church in Plano, TX, with the subject of the debate being “Why I am/am not a Christian”.

Hopefully you will enjoy it as much as I did!
