Martyn Lloyd Jones Resources

Audio sermons a plenty...

I am quite the fan of listening to podcasts and whatnot as I drive around in my van, and I have recently been listening to various Dr. Martyn Lloyd Jones audio sermons. These are quite brilliant, and highly recommended.

The MLJ Trust has made available over 1600 recorded sermons from the great man himself, all for free.

There is also a mobile app available, which makes the collections easy to navigate, has a built in player, and has a significant introduction to each sermon.

Martyn Lloyd Jones will be one of the first few people I am going to write a short bio about on this site, for now here is an example of a one of his quotation…

“Prayer is beyond any question the highest activity of the human soul. Man is at his greatest and highest when upon his knees he comes face to face with God.”― Martyn Lloyd-Jones

I hope you find these resources as enjoyable as I have. If so, please consider visiting the trust to make a donation so they can continue to share these quite amazing resources.

#Martin Lloyd Jones
